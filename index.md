# Orb — The Assistant That Talks First

Orb is a voice-first AI you actually *own*. You name it, you shape its personality,
and you decide what brain runs behind it. The AI model is just an engine in the
settings menu — the product is the assistant that gets things done in your life.

That's the whole idea: **the backend disappears.** Once it's set up, you don't think
about which model is answering. You talk to Orb, Orb helps, and it sounds the same
whether it's running fully offline on your phone, on a free API, or on a server you
host yourself.

## What makes it different

- **Bring your own brain.** On-device, free APIs, or your own machine — your choice,
  swappable any time. No single company is the point.
- **Always works.** Offline it runs on-device. If a capability isn't available right
  now, Orb tells you plainly instead of failing silently.
- **One personality, every backend.** Switch the engine and the voice stays *your*
  Orb. Models are invisible infrastructure.
- **Self-hostable.** Run the optional Orb server on your own PC for the most power —
  and connect to it from anywhere.
- **Private by default.** On-device data stays on your device. A server you run is
  *yours*; you only ever connect to your own.
